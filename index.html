    About Pixelate

    Pixelate is a new kid on the block, and the whole neighbourhood is better for it...

    Who We Are

    Created in Charlottetown PEI, Pixelate is a web service company designed to help navigate the world of business on the internet.

    How We Operate

    Our business model is one that represents the clients interest first. This means being available at any time, for any request, for any client.

    Our Passion

    We see a future where all business lives on the internet, at least to some degree. Our goal is to ensure that no business is left behind, or stuck in the stone age.

    Why Us?

    We understand that this is new to your business, and we want to be the helping hand. From websites to emerging technologies such as Web3, we can be the voice between you and your computers.
