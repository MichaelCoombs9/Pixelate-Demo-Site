    About Pixelate

    Pixelate is a new kid on the block, and the whole neighbourhood is better for it...

    Who We Are

    Created in Charlottetown PEI, Pixelate is a web service company designed to help navigate the world of business on the internet.

    How We Operate

    Our business model is one that represents the clients interest first. This means being available at any time, for any request, for any client.

    Our Passion

    We see a future where all business lives on the internet, at least to some degree. Our goal is to ensure that no business is left behind, or stuck in the stone age.

    Why Us?

    We understand that this is new to your business, and we want to be the helping hand. From websites to emerging technologies such as Web3, we can be the voice between you and your computers.

    Services

    Here at Pixelate we offer a wide range of services to accommodate your business in any way you need. Our services cover everything your business needs to join the online world in the best way possible.

    Web Development

    Our web development team specializes in designing and creating the perfect website for your business needs. A responsive website is the focal point of your business, and requires nothing less than the best.

    App Development

    Our development team offers app development for both IOS and Android devices. We can provide a functionally designed app to best operate your business, and showcase it on the App Store.

    Web Hosting

    Rather than using a hosting site with an overloaded server in another continent, here at Pixelate we offer the chance to host your site with our own servers. This means your business will relieve the best connection, maintenance, and networking.

    Consulting

    Our consulting team is here to help you best understand the tech world in relation to your business. Specialized in new technologies, such as Web3, our consult team will be a sounding board and think tank for taking your business to the next level.

    Social Media Marketing

    Our marketing team specializes in social media ad management, and design. As the most effective means of advertisement, our team will provide well-designed advertisements to reach only your targeted audience

    Blockchain & Cryptocurrencies

    The world of Blockchain and Cryptocurrencies is growing everyday. Our team at Pixelate is here to open your business to that world with the use of NFTs, Crypto Tokens, and Web3 services.
